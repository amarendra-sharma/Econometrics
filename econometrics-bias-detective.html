What you just learned

"Controlling for" is arithmetic, not magic: adding a confounder removes the part of the education coefficient that was carrying the confounder's effect, and the direction of the correction is the sign of (its effect on y) × (its association with x). Ability biased the return up, experience biased it down (negative association, positive effect), region nudged it, and parental income — with no direct wage effect in this DGP — barely mattered. You can now sign a bias before touching data, which is exactly what referees do to your paper.

★ Sharma's Take

The detective's habit: before every interrogation, write the two signs in the margin. If you cannot sign them, you do not yet understand your own confounder story.
