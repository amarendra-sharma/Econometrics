What you just learned

The within transformation is not a black box: it is literally subtracting each firm's own averages, and you watched it happen geometrically — clusters collapsing onto a common origin, the between-firm confounding evaporating, the slope flipping from the seductive pooled lie to the within truth. Fixed effects buys immunity to every time-constant confounder; what it cannot fix (time-varying confounders, anything you care about that never changes) you also now understand, because you saw exactly which variation survives the x-ray.

★ Sharma's Take

When pooled and FE disagree violently, do not average them — ask what time-constant trait could drive both x and y across units. The disagreement is the diagnosis.
