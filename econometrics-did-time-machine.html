What you just learned

DiD is a transplant operation: it grafts the control group's trend onto the treated group as the counterfactual, and the estimate is the treated group's departure from that graft. The whole design lives on parallel trends — and you watched a pre-trend dial quietly poison the estimate while the 2×2 arithmetic kept smiling. The event study is the detector: flat leads keep the design alive; sloped leads are the forgery showing through.

★ Sharma's Take

No event-study figure, no trust — that is the modern referee's reflex, and now it is yours. Always show the leads.
