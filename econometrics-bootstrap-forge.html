What you just learned

The sampling distribution is a physical thing you can build with your hands: resample, re-estimate, stack. In the clean world your forged histogram matched the normal curve and the classical SE almost exactly — the formula is a shortcut for the hammering. In the broken world the shortcut failed and the hammering did not: the (pairs) bootstrap tracks the estimator's true variability when the textbook's variance assumption dies, which is why it is the practitioner's check whenever formulas feel doubtful.
